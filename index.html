FAQ

Quick answers
Is this only for Jamaicans?

It’s Caribbean-centered and welcomes the Caribbean diaspora too. Shared culture + shared faith are the focus.

Is this casual dating?

No. This platform is marriage-focused. Courtship is approached with clarity, respect, and intention.

Do I have to be “perfect”?

No. Sincerity matters. This is a space for growth and maturity — not performance.

How do conversation prompts work?

Prompts are optional and meant to reduce awkwardness and encourage meaningful discussion. You can ignore them any time.
