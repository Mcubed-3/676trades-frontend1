Trading Settings

Recommended for beginners: Start with XAUUSD and 0.01 lot size. Save settings, then start the algo once your EA shows Connected.
i Enter symbols separated by commas. Example: XAUUSD,EURUSD. Use only symbols available on your broker.
Keep it simple: start with 1–2 pairs.
i Trade volume per position. Smaller = lower risk. Example: 0.01 is a common starting size.
i Dynamic = EA calculates SL from price structure. Fixed = uses your Fixed SL pips (advanced).
i RR = targets a multiple of risk (simple + popular). Fixed/Pattern are advanced options.
i RR=1 means TP distance equals SL distance. RR=2 targets double the SL distance.
If you’re unsure, keep RR at 1.0.
