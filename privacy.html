Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how 676Trades (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services (the “Service”).

1) What we collect

  • Account info: email address, password hash (we do not store your plaintext password).
  • API key: a key that links your EA to your account (treat it like a password).
  • Billing status: plan/subscription status and IDs from our payment processor (e.g., customer/subscription IDs).
  • EA connectivity: heartbeat “last seen” time, and may include symbol/timeframe and basic connection metadata.
  • Trading configuration: enabled/disabled state and settings you configure in the dashboard.
  • Logs: basic security/diagnostic logs such as IP address and request metadata (for abuse prevention and debugging).

2) What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your broker password.
  • We do not have direct access to withdraw funds from your broker account.
  • We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

3) How we use your information

  • To provide the Service, authenticate users, and deliver dashboard/EA functionality.
  • To enforce plan access (e.g., trial/subscription gating where applicable).
  • To detect fraud/abuse and protect the platform.
  • To provide support and troubleshoot issues.

4) Payments (third-party processors)

Payments are processed by third parties (for example, Stripe). We do not store full card details. Payment processors handle your payment information under their own policies.

5) Cookies / local storage

The Service may use browser storage (e.g., localStorage/sessionStorage) to keep you logged in and to store session data. You can clear your browser data to remove this.

6) Sharing your information

We may share data only when necessary:

  • With payment processors to manage billing/subscriptions.
  • With infrastructure providers used to host the Service.
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and users.

7) Data retention

We retain account and service data as long as needed to operate the Service, comply with obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion where applicable, subject to legal requirements.

8) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. Keep your API key private and rotate it if compromised.

9) International users

You may access the Service from different countries. Your data may be processed in the regions where our providers operate. By using the Service, you consent to this processing where permitted by law.

10) Contact

If you have questions or requests (access, correction, deletion), contact: support@676trades.org
