Community Guidelines

This platform exists to make connection feel safe, respectful, and not intimidating. We’re building a community for Caribbean Christian singles who desire marriage and want to interact with dignity and clarity.

Quick summary
Be kind. Be honest. No sexual content. No pressure. No manipulation. If someone says “no” or shows disinterest, accept it respectfully and move on.

1) What we’re building

This is not swipe culture. It’s a Christ-centered environment where community comes first, and courtship happens with intention.

  • Community first: Groups & discussion reduce pressure and help people socialize naturally.
  • Marriage-focused: We’re not here for casual dating or hookups.
  • Respectful conduct: How we speak matters as much as what we want.

2) Who this is for

People with different personalities are welcome here — quiet, reflective, expressive, outgoing. The standard is sincerity and respect.

  • Christian singles who desire marriage and intentional courtship
  • Caribbean and Caribbean diaspora (UK/Canada/US and beyond)
  • People who want a calm space without intimidation

Core Rules (Non-Negotiable)

If you break these, you may be warned, restricted, or removed.

Be respectful

No insults, humiliation, belittling, harassment, or aggressive messages. Speak as you would in a healthy Christian community.

No sexual content

No explicit messages, sexual pressure, sexual requests, or inappropriate photos. This includes “testing boundaries” or pushing.

No pressure or coercion

No guilt-tripping, manipulation, repeated messaging after disinterest, or emotional pressure to get a response.

Honesty & integrity

No impersonation. No scams. No pretending to be someone else. If you’re unsure, be honest.

Important: If someone isn’t interested, accept it with maturity. Rejection is not a reason to insult, argue, or persist.

How to behave in groups & chats

Tap each section to expand.

Group discussions
Groups are for community and faith-aligned discussion — not hunting or pressuring people.
  • Stay respectful even when you disagree.
  • No shaming, mocking, or “calling out” people publicly.
  • No spam, ads, or constant self-promotion.
  • Don’t try to push people into private chat aggressively.
Private messages
Private chat should feel safe and calm. Keep it meaningful.
  • Start with respectful conversation, not demands.
  • Don’t ask for intimate photos or sexual talk.
  • If the person is slow to respond, don’t pressure them.
  • If someone says “no” — accept it and move on.
Faith and disagreements
This platform is Christian. But we still keep conversations peaceful.
  • No condemning or “holier-than-thou” attacks.
  • No forcing debates in comments or DMs.
  • Speak with humility. Ask questions. Keep it kind.
Personal information & boundaries
Safety matters. Don’t rush or pressure.
  • Do not demand phone numbers, addresses, or location details.
  • Don’t pressure someone to meet quickly.
  • Respect privacy and comfort levels.

Reporting & enforcement

Our goal is not to punish — it’s to protect the community and keep the environment safe and respectful.

What you can report

  • Harassment, insults, threats
  • Sexual pressure or inappropriate content
  • Scams, fake identities, suspicious behavior
  • Repeated unwanted messages

What may happen

  • Warning and education
  • Temporary restrictions
  • Removal from groups
  • Account suspension or permanent ban
